Honey and Lemon for Cough

Respiratory Antimicrobial

Ingredients: 2 tbsp raw honey, 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice, warm water

Preparation: Mix honey and lemon in warm water. Drink 2-3 times daily.

Benefits: Soothes throat irritation, reduces cough, boosts immune system.

(4.8/5)

Ginger Tea for Nausea

Digestive Anti-nausea

Ingredients: 1 inch fresh ginger root, 1 cup hot water, honey to taste

Preparation: Slice ginger, steep in hot water for 10 minutes, strain and add honey.

Benefits: Reduces nausea, aids digestion, anti-inflammatory properties.

(4.9/5)

Turmeric Golden Milk

Anti-inflammatory Pain Relief

Ingredients: 1 tsp turmeric powder, 1 cup warm milk, pinch of black pepper, honey

Preparation: Mix turmeric and pepper in warm milk, sweeten with honey.

Benefits: Reduces inflammation, relieves joint pain, improves sleep quality.

(4.7/5)

Aloe Vera for Skin Healing

Skin Care Healing

Ingredients: Fresh aloe vera gel (from plant leaf)

Preparation: Extract gel from aloe leaf, apply directly to affected area.

Benefits: Heals burns, soothes irritation, moisturizes dry skin.

(4.6/5)
